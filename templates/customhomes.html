Five things to consider when investing in a custom home.

CONTEMPLATE WHAT YOU WANT IN A HOME, THEN CALL TURN KEY CONSTRUCTION!

Building a custom home is a process. There are a few factors to consider before our crews break ground. Contact the professionals at Turn Key Construction for assistance with any aspect of planning your new custom home.

  1. Budget -- How much you want to fiscally invest in your home will influence many features. We can help adjust your floor plan or amenities to accommodate any budget.
  2. Location -- The location of your lot may drastically alter the layout of your home. The landscape, proximity to neighbors and access to sunlight are elements that may influence the custom look and function of your home.
  3. Family size/special accommodations -- Your family’s composition can impact the size and layout of your custom home. The quantity and positioning of bedrooms are greatly affected by family size and make-up. Another factor to consider is age. A single-story home may be more practical for elderly family members.
  4. New design or plan alterations -- Do you want to start completely from scratch or alter an existing floor plan? Complete designs can be more complicated but they give you total control over all aspects of your home. Going with an existing plan means your home will somewhat resemble another home, but you still have free range to alter room dimensions, window styles and arrangements and interior features to customize your home.
